Our Story

Built for
Experience Seekers

EventSphere was born from a simple idea — every extraordinary experience deserves an audience. Founded in 2026, we connect passionate event organizers with curious attendees across India.

From intimate art exhibitions to massive music festivals, our platform makes discovery, booking and management effortless. We believe great memories start with great tools.

Our platform is built on three pillars:

  • Discovery — Intelligent filtering by category, city, date and price makes finding your next event effortless.
  • Trust — Stripe-powered payments, verified organizers and real attendee reviews ensure every booking is safe and authentic.
  • Community — Post-event feedback, ratings and social sharing turn individual experiences into shared memories.

Whether you're an attendee looking for the next unforgettable night, or an organizer building your audience — EventSphere is your home.
